# Directives: title

Abbreviation: `t`.

This directive defines the title of the song.

Examples:

    {title: Swing Low Sweet Chariot}
    {meta: title Swing Low Sweet Chariot}
    {t: Swing Low Sweet Chariot}

See also: [meta]({{< relref "Directives-meta" >}}).
